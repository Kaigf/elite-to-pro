Mission

Through our small dedicated team, we are seeking to revolutionize the video game industry by supporting the growth of eSports. Elite to Pro was founded with the intentions of connecting gamers to one another both locally and globally by creating a community driven platform for elite gamers to utilize. Our goal is to support every elite gamer in their quest for finding the right teammates, to create the dream team, and achieve the desired status as a pro gamer.

Stay Connected:

We are here to help gamers connect and find their dream teams, compete, and continue to grow. Check out our twitch channel and Facebook page.
